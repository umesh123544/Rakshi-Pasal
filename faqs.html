Why do I need to show ID for delivery?

By law, we cannot deliver alcohol to anyone under 21 years of age. Our delivery personnel will verify your age with a valid government-issued ID upon delivery. This is required for every delivery, even if you've ordered before.

Do I need an account to place an order?

No, you can place orders as a guest. However, creating an account lets you:

  • Track your order history
  • Save favorite products
  • Get exclusive offers
  • Faster checkout

How are perishable items handled?

All perishable items are stored in temperature-controlled environments and delivered in insulated packaging when necessary. If you have specific requirements, please mention them in the order notes.
