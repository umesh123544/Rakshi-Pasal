Return Policy

Our Return Policy

At Aaila Pasa, we strive for 100% customer satisfaction. If you're not completely happy with your purchase, we'll make it right.

Due to the nature of our products, we can only accept returns for damaged or incorrect items.

Eligible Returns

We accept returns for the following reasons:

Damaged products (broken seals, leaks)

Incorrect items delivered

Changed your mind

Ordered wrong product

Return Timeframe

To be eligible for a return:

1

Report Immediately

Notify us within 1 hour of delivery

2

Return Window

Return must be completed within 24 hours

Returns are subject to inspection by our team.

Return Process

Follow these steps to return an item:

  1. Contact us immediately at +977 9813197704 or via WhatsApp
  2. Provide photos of the damaged/incorrect item
  3. Keep all items in original condition (do not open)
  4. Wait for instructions from our customer service team

Refund Policy

Once your return is approved:

Replacement

Free replacement of the same product

Refund

Full refund to original payment method (processed within 3-5 business days)

Original delivery fee is non-refundable.

Non-Returnable Items

For safety and legal reasons, we cannot accept returns for:

Opened alcohol bottles

Products with broken seals

Items damaged by improper storage
