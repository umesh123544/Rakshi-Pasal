Age Verification

By law, we cannot deliver alcohol to anyone under 21 years of age. Our delivery personnel will verify your age with a valid government-issued ID upon delivery.

If you cannot provide valid ID, we will cancel your order and issue a refund minus any delivery fees.

Failed Deliveries

If we're unable to complete delivery due to:

  • Incorrect address
  • Recipient unavailable
  • Refusal to show ID
  • Recipient appears intoxicated

We will attempt to contact you to resolve the issue. If delivery cannot be completed, we will issue a refund minus any delivery fees.
